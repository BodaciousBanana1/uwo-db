The town, located in the western part of Germany, grew as the center of the Holy Roman Empire. It is said the name originated in the 5th century as the place for the Franks to ford the Main river. St. Bartholomew's Cathedral was appointed the location of the Imperial Elections and the Coronation ceremony.
