There's a request for immigration to your Company Colony from a pharmacist called Haru who currently lives in Sakai. However, in preparation for moving, she requires 20 boxes of a fruit called Noni. You should be able to purchase Noni in Ternate.. When you deliver it to Sakai, you can pass it onto Yuki from the Tavern.
