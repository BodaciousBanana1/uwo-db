In order to increase the number of immigration to the Company Colony, it is important to provide sufficient aid to those who are planning to immigrate. Try to deliver any arms-type Trade Goods to the Market Keeper of Casablanca, who has agreed to help us in this matter. Apparently, as the degree of the Delivery Progress get higher, you will be able to receive better rewards. Also, just for you information, there seems to be some arms-type Trade Goods that can increase the Delivery Progress more easily than others regardless of the amount delivered.
