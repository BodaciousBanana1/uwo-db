To make the traffic to and from the Company Colony smooth and safe, please investigate the terrain from the Northeast Atlantic Ocean. Perform Recognition at the designated point, and make your reports. If your fleet members who are undertaking the same request haven't investigated that point yet, you can share the investigation result with them if you all are in the same sea area.
